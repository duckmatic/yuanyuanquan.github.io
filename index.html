About My Research

My PhD project explored the impact of pollinator declines on plant community and ecosystem functioning from a landscape perspective. Specifically, I looked at different land-use intensities and how pollinators interact with other plant antagonists such as herbivores and seed predators.

I combine both manipulative and observational approaches with multi-year experiments in grassland systems (e.g., within Skåne province). My background includes a Bachelor's degree in Forestry and an MSc degree in Ecology.
